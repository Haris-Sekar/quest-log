# Gym Plan — 150kg → 90kg

**The real enemy isn't the workout — it's the 2-weeks-and-quit pattern.** So this plan optimizes for *showing up*, not for intensity. Intensity comes later; it can't help you if you're not in the building.

**Hard rule at your current weight: no running, no jumping, no jerky lifts.** Your knees and ankles are carrying 150kg — protect them. Walking, incline treadmill, cycling, rowing and machines are your world until ~120kg. This is not a soft option; it's the smart one.

## Phase 0 — "Just Show Up" (Weeks 1–2)

Goal: rebuild the habit. Nothing else matters.

- **3 days/week** — Mon / Wed / Sat (lock the days, put them in your calendar)
- Each session, max 40 minutes:
  - 10 min treadmill walk (comfortable pace)
  - 5 machines × 2 light sets × 12 reps (pick any — leg press, chest press, lat pulldown, seated row, leg curl)
  - 10 min treadmill walk, slight incline
- Leave feeling like you could have done more. **That's the point.** Soreness kills streaks.
- Non-gym days: one 30-min walk (evening, after dinner).

✅ Success = 6 sessions in 2 weeks. Weight lost: irrelevant this phase.

## Phase 1 — Build the Base (Weeks 3–8)

**3 days/week, full body, machines:**

| Exercise | Sets × Reps |
|---|---|
| Leg press | 3 × 12 |
| Chest press machine | 3 × 12 |
| Lat pulldown | 3 × 12 |
| Seated row | 3 × 10 |
| Leg curl | 3 × 12 |
| Plank (knees OK) or dead-bug | 3 × 30 sec |

- Finish with **20 min incline treadmill walk** (incline 6–10, speed easy, you should be able to talk).
- **Progression:** when 12 reps feels easy on all 3 sets → +2.5–5kg next session. Log your weights in a notes app.
- Off days: walking. Build toward **8,000 steps/day** (pace calls, park farther, walk the office basement loops).

## Phase 2 — The Grind (Months 3–6)

**4 days/week, upper/lower split:**

- **Upper (Tue/Fri):** chest press, lat pulldown, seated row, shoulder press machine, dumbbell curls, triceps pushdown — 3×10–12
- **Lower (Mon/Thu):** leg press, leg curl, leg extension, calf raise, cable pull-through, core — 3×10–12
- Cardio: 25–30 min after each session (incline walk / cycle / rower — rotate)
- Steps: **10,000/day**
- By now you'll be down 12–18kg and everything feels easier. This is where most people finally start *enjoying* it.

## Phase 3 — Months 6+ (reassess at ~125–130kg)

- Introduce free weights: goblet squats, dumbbell bench, dumbbell rows
- Add a fun cardio: badminton, swimming, cycling — something you'd do even if it didn't burn calories
- Keep lifting 3–4×/week as weight drops — **strength training is what makes you lose fat instead of fat + muscle.** At 90kg you want to look strong, not deflated. Wedding photos, remember.

## Streak Insurance (read when motivation dips)

- **The 10-minute rule:** on a "can't be bothered" day, go and do only the treadmill for 10 minutes. You're allowed to leave after. (You almost never will.)
- **Never miss twice.** Missing one session is life. Missing two in a row is the old pattern restarting.
- Gym at a **fixed time** — right after office works best (you're already out; home = gaming gravity well).
- Your last attempt died at 2 weeks. **Day 15 is the real boss fight.** The tracker app has an achievement waiting for you there.
