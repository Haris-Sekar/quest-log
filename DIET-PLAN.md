# Diet Plan — 150kg → 90kg (Office Canteen Edition)

**Who:** 24M, 175cm, 150kg, software engineer (9–6 desk job), all meals from office canteen.
**Target:** 90kg in 18–24 months (wedding timeline). That's ~0.6–0.8 kg/week average — aggressive early, slower later. Realistic.

> ⚠️ At 150kg, get a basic checkup first (BP, fasting sugar/HbA1c, thyroid, lipids). Weight this high often has metabolic passengers — knowing your numbers makes the plan safer and shows progress the scale can't.

## The Math

| | |
|---|---|
| Maintenance (TDEE) at 150kg, sedentary | ~3,100–3,300 kcal/day |
| Your eating target | **1,800–2,000 kcal/day** |
| Daily deficit | ~1,200 kcal → ~1–1.2 kg/week early on |
| Protein target | 110–130g/day (protects muscle, kills hunger) |

Expected curve: **−12 to −15kg in the first 3 months** (some is water — enjoy it, it's motivating), then ~3–4kg/month, reaching ~90kg around month 20–24.

## The 6 Rules (this IS the diet)

1. **One plate, no seconds.** Serve once, walk away from the counter.
2. **Protein at every meal.** Boiled egg / omelette / dal / curd — every single meal. Ask for extra boiled eggs; nobody minds.
3. **Fried food is a weekend boss, not a daily mob.** Poori, vada, appalam, murukku, fried rice — max ONE treat, once a week.
4. **Rice is measured, not mounded.** ~200g cooked rice item per meal (roughly one standard canteen ladle + half).
5. **Drink before you eat.** 1 glass of water before every meal; 3L+ total per day. Buttermilk > juice, always.
6. **Sugar off in tea/coffee.** Ginger tea without sugar is 15 kcal, with sugar it's 60+ — and you drink 3 a day.

## How to Read Any Day's Menu

The menu changes daily, so learn the pattern, not the items:

| ✅ Default picks | ⚠️ Portion-controlled | ❌ Skip (fried/sugar bombs) |
|---|---|---|
| Boiled egg, omelette | Rice items — 200g max | Poori, vada, bonda, bajji |
| Sambar, rasam, dal/kootu | Idli ×3 / dosa ×2 / chapathi ×2 | Butter murukku (563 kcal/100g!) |
| Veg poriyal / palya / kootu | Pongal (ghee-heavy) | Appalam, fried rice, kaldosa+butter |
| Buttermilk, curd | Coconut chutney (2 spoons) | Payasam, sweets |
| Boiled sweet potato, banana | Ragi malt (no sugar) | Juice, sugary tea |

## Protein Gap

Canteen gets you ~80–95g. To hit 110g+: **1 scoop whey in water/milk at night** (buy a 1kg tub, any standard brand). This single habit is worth more than any supplement aisle browsing.

## Habits That Multiply the Diet

- **10-min walk after lunch and dinner.** Non-negotiable — best glucose/fat hack available for free.
- **Eat dinner in the 7:00–8:00pm window**, not 10pm. Late eating + gaming + sleep is the pattern that got here.
- **Sleep 7h.** Sleep deprivation = hunger hormones up = willpower gone. In bed by 11:30.
- **Weigh weekly** (same day, morning, after toilet, before food). Daily weight noise will mess with your head.

## When You Slip (you will)

One bad meal ≠ a bad day. One bad day ≠ a bad week. The rule is simple: **never miss twice.** Slip, log it, next meal is back on plan. Guilt is not part of this program.
